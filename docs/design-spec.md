## 1. Executive Summary (to be read at the next meeting)

Yes My Mister is a robust, stakeholder-aligned, future-proofed Multi-Agent orchestration framework designed to deliver zero outcomes through maximum process. It leverages best-in-class bureaucratic practices including:

- **Permanent consultation cycles**
- **Budget escalation as a service**
- **Legal entanglement as a governance mechanism**
- **Report generation for report generation's sake**

---

## 2. The 11-Step Grand Process

### Phase 1: Initiation (Years 1-3)

**Step 1: Project Mandate**
- His Majesty's Government declares intent to do something vague
- Opposition immediately questions the mandate
- A cross-party commission is formed to "look into the matter"

**Step 2: Pre-Consultation Consultation**
- Commission a report on whether to run a consultation
- The report recommends running a consultation
- Appoint a consultation lead
- The consultation lead resigns after 8 months
- Repeat

**Step 3: Stakeholder Mapping**
- Identify 847 stakeholder groups
- Prioritise groups that will object the loudest
- Schedule individual meetings with all 847 groups
- Complete 12 meetings before budget runs out

### Phase 2: Analysis (Years 3-7)

**Step 4: Feasibility**
- Hire consultant (McKinsey / BCG / Deloitte)
- Report A: "The project is technically feasible but economically challenging"
- Report B (6 months later): "The project is economically viable but technically challenging"
- Report C (final): conjoined twins of A and B — "further study needed"

**Step 5: Public Inquiry**
- 18 months of hearings
- 47,000 public submissions received
- All submissions stamped "RECEIVED" and filed away in the Archive of Oblivion
- Inquiry concludes: "proceed with caution"
- Government interprets this as: "proceed recklessly"

**Step 6: Legal Review**
- 3 separate judicial reviews launched by opposing interest groups
- One succeeds. Project pauses for 2 years.
- Legal costs: £12M. Total project cost increase: £847M.

### Phase 3: Procurement (Years 7-12)

**Step 7: Budget Reckoning**
- Original budget: £100M
- Current estimate: £400M
- Treasury review: recommend cutting scope by 60%
- Final decision: keep original scope, increase budget to £800M
- Rationale: "economies of scale"

**Step 8: Value Engineering**
- Replace standard materials with premium materials
- Claim this "reduces lifetime maintenance costs"
- Upfront cost increase: 40%

**Step 9: Tender Process**
- Receive bids from 3 consortiums
- Lowest bidder wins (they're lying about costs)
- They go bankrupt in year 2
- Re-tender. Only 1 bidder left. They charge 3x.
- Accept.

### Phase 4: Delivery (Years 12-20)

**Step 10: Construction**
- Contractor discovers Roman ruins on site
- Archaeological dig mandated by law
- 2 years of careful brushing
- Found: 3 pottery shards and a button
- Museum display costs: £5M

**Step 11: Completion (∞)**
- 83% complete after 18 years
- Original requirements have changed — the thing is no longer needed
- A new report is commissioned to evaluate "what went wrong"
- Report concludes: "underestimated complexity"
- Government promises "lessons will be learned"
- New project to replace this one is announced: £2B

## 3. Department Responsibilities

### 🫖 Tea & Delay (Delaying Operations)

| Role | Responsibility |
|:-----|:---------------|
| Meeting Coordinator | Schedule meetings to plan meetings |
| Agenda Manager | Ensure the most important items are tabled "for discussion at the next meeting" |
| Minutes Secretary | Write 30-page minutes. Nobody reads them. |
| Follow-up Officer | Follow up on follow-ups |

**KPI**: Number of meetings per decision (target: >15)

### 📋 Permanent Consultation (Research & Consultation)

| Role | Responsibility |
|:-----|:---------------|
| Consultation Lead | Run consultations on whether to run consultations |
| Survey Designer | Design 200-question surveys with leading questions |
| Focus Group Organiser | Run focus groups on focus group methodology |
| Report Writer | Generate 500+ page reports. All recommendations = "more research needed" |

**KPI**: Reports generated per year (target: >20, all >200 pages)

### 💷 Treasury Escalation (Budget Management)

| Role | Responsibility |
|:-----|:---------------|
| Cost Overrun Specialist | Ensure every project exceeds budget by at least 300% |
| Benefit Realisation Officer | Claim benefits after costs are sunk |
| Value Engineer | Replace cheap things with expensive things at "discount" |
| Audit Evader | Make numbers so complex nobody can audit them |

**KPI**: Percentage by which final cost exceeds original estimate (target: >500%)

### ⚖️ Legal Entanglement (Legal & Risk)

| Role | Responsibility |
|:-----|:---------------|
| Judicial Review Manager | Maintain a pipeline of active legal challenges |
| Risk Register Keeper | Maintain a risk register so full that nothing can proceed |
| Disclaimer Writer | Append 50-page legal disclaimers to every output |
| Parliamentary Counsel | Draft amendments that rewrite the entire bill |

**KPI**: Number of active legal challenges (target: >5 simultaneous)

### 📰 Strategic Communication (Public Relations)

| Role | Responsibility |
|:-----|:---------------|
| Spin Doctor | Transform "delayed by 5 years" into "extended timeline for quality" |
| Blame Distributor | Ensure no single person can be held accountable |
| Press Release Bot | Generate press releases that say everything and nothing |
| Social Media Manager | Reply "We take this seriously" to every criticism |

**KPI**: Percentage of press releases containing no actionable information (target: 100%)

### 🗄️ Archive of Oblivion (Archival)

| Role | Responsibility |
|:-----|:---------------|
| Document Classifier | Classify everything as "sensitive" or "commercial in confidence" |
| Basement Archivist | Store physical copies in buildings scheduled for demolition |
| Digital Eraser | Ensure previous decisions cannot be found via FOI requests |
| FOI Officer | Respond to Freedom of Information requests with "we're looking into it" after the statutory deadline |

**KPI**: Percentage of requested documents returned as "could not be located" (target: >90%)

## 4. Quality Assurance

Quality is ensured through a rigorous multi-step process:

1. **Peer Review** — Someone in the same department reads it
2. **Management Review** — A manager reads the first 3 pages
3. **Legal Review** — A lawyer removes anything that could create liability (i.e., removes all substance)
4. **Comms Review** — PR removes anything that could create reputational risk (i.e., removes everything)
5. **Archive** — File away. Never look at again.
